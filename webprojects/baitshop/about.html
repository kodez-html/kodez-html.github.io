Our Story

At The Fish Store, we believe in providing the best for our customers. Our journey began with a simple lad in the Pacific Northwest who loved fishing and wanted to share that passion with his community. Today, we continue that tradition by offering fresh live bait and quality fishing supplies to anglers of all levels.

Family-Owned. Community-Driven.

From our first minnow tank to our full general store, our goal has always been to keep the local fishing tradition alive. Whether you're gearing up for a tournament or spending a quiet weekend at the lake, we’ve got what you need.
